Research

Dr. Landau's research focuses on the neural and cognitive changes that occur in aging, mild cognitive impairment, and Alzheimer’s disease. Recent work with the Alzheimer’s Disease Neuroimaging Initiative (ADNI) has exmined the predictive role of amyloid PET and FDG-PET imaging, and other genetic, cerebrospinal fluid, and imaging biomarkers in cognitive decline. An additional research area is the contribution of lifestyle factors, such as cognitive stimulation, to aging and amyloid deposition, and the impact of chemotherapy on neural and cognitive function.
